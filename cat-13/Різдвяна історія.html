Різдвяна Історія

People wake up on December 24. The store was overflowing with Christmas goods, and there was a Christmas tree in the main square. Children wrote letters to Santa Claus, and adults prepared Christmas dinner. When everyone sat down at the table on the Christmas tree garland, the lights went out, and a black cloud hung over the houses and black snow fell. When people came to the square, they were upset and thought that Christmas might be ruined. But among all the people stood a family with a little girl who knew a way out of the situation. She went to the tree and looked at it with her little eyes, after that the clouds dissolved into the air and Christmas continued.
